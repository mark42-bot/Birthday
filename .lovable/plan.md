

## Romantic Birthday Wishing Site 🎂💕

A fun & playful birthday site for your girlfriend/boyfriend with bright colors, confetti, and bouncy animations.

### Pages & Flow
1. **Welcome Screen** — Animated entrance with a "Click to Open" gift box or envelope animation that transitions to the main content
2. **Main Birthday Page** — Scrollable single-page experience with all sections below

### Sections
1. **Hero** — Big animated "Happy Birthday [Name]!" with floating hearts, confetti burst, and a fun birthday cake emoji animation
2. **Love Letter** — A styled card/letter with a heartfelt message area (you can customize the text), with a handwritten-style font and a subtle unfold animation
3. **Photo Gallery** — A playful photo carousel/grid to showcase your favorite moments together (placeholder images you can replace with your own via chat)
4. **Background Music** — A floating music player button to play/pause a romantic song (will use an audio file you can swap out)

### Design
- **Colors**: Warm pinks, coral, soft purples, and playful accents
- **Fonts**: Playful handwritten-style for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- **Animations**: Confetti on load, floating hearts, bouncy entrance animations, hover effects on photos
- **Responsive**: Works on both mobile and desktop

### Personalization
- Easy-to-edit name, messages, and photos directly in the code
- You can upload your own photos and music through chat after the site is built
